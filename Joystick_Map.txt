// Joystick map.txt

CARM - Cargo arm
HARM - Height Attainment arm

OPERATOR joystick
4 - HARM jog up
5 - HARM jog down
axis 2 - CARM jog up
axis 3 - CARM jog down 
3 - HARM up 1 position
1 - HARM down 1 position
2 - CARM to up position
0 - CARM to down position
7 - CARM to start position
push 9 - climb

DRIVER joystick
axis 1 (left stick Y) - arcade drive forward/backward
axis 4 - (right stick X) - arcade drive left/right
axis 3 - jacks down
axis 2 - jacks up
4 - CARM rollers out
5 - CARM rollers in fast
2 - CARM rollers in slow
3 - vacuum in
1 - vacuum out
0 - auto (camera) align
rumble - after ball secured, duration 2 seconds
rumble - 200 milliseconds after vacuum out, duration 2 seconds
