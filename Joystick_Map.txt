// Joystick map.txt

CARM - Cargo arm
HARM - Height Attainment arm

OPERATOR joystick
4 - HARM jog up
5 - HARM jog down
axis 2 - CARM jog up
axis 3 - CARM jog down 
3 - HARM up 1 position (see HARM POSITIONS section)
1 - HARM down 1 position (see HARM POSITIONS section)
2 - CARM to climb position
0 - CARM to intake position
7 - CARM to start/store position
push 9 - auto climb

DRIVER joystick
axis 1 (left stick Y) - arcade drive forward/backward
axis 4 - (right stick X) - arcade drive left/right
axis 3 - jacks down
axis 2 - jacks up
4 - CARM rollers out
5 - CARM rollers in fast
2 - CARM rollers in slow
3 - vacuum in
1 - vacuum out
0 - auto (camera) align
rumble - after ball secured, duration 2 seconds
rumble - 200 milliseconds after vacuum out, duration 2 seconds

HARM POSITIONS (all positions are based on center of cargo hatch / cargo pod distance above ground)
pod loading ?? (all the way down)
hatch loading station / cargo hatch 1 / rocket hatch 1: 19"
rocket port 1: 27.5"
rocket hatch 2: 47"
rocket port 2: 56.5"
