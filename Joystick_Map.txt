OPERATOR joystick
5 - HARM jog up (old 4)
6 - HARM jog down (old 5)

axis 3 - CARM jog down (old axis 3)

1 - CARM to intake position (old 0)
8 - CARM to start/store position (old 7)

DRIVER joystick

axis 4 - (right stick X) - arcade drive left/right (old axis 4)
axis 3 - jacks down (old axis 3)
axis 2 - jacks up (old axis 2)
5 - CARM rollers out  (old 4)
6 - CARM rollers in fast (old 5)
3 - CARM rollers in slow (old 2)
7 - hatch vacuum on (old 6)
8 - pod vacuum on (old 7)
11? (big silver button in center) - blow vacuum (old 10)
1 - auto (camera) align (old 0)
rumble - after ball secured, duration 2 seconds
rumble - 200 milliseconds after vacuum out, duration 2 seconds
